About Us

McDowell Surveyors is an independent Marine and Cargo Survey Firm that provides quality services to the Maritime Industry, serving local and international customers throughout Florida & The Caribbean.

Established in Dominican Republic in 1969, our many years of experience in the maritime field makes us confident to provide efficient and rapid response to your needs in any situation.

In 2017, McDowell Surveyors incorporated regional offices in Florida, to provide full services and faster response to the US Market as well as ports all around the Caribbean.

OUR VISION

To be the Firm of choice for Marine Surveying, Cargo Inspection and Maritime Consultancy Services in the Caribbean Region.

OUR MISSION

To provide the best services and contribute towards reducing risks and liability exposure through accurate and consistent expertise.

OUR PRINCIPLES

Honesty, professionalism and dedication have guided McDowell Surveyors since its incorporation; Understanding the key importance of a good outcome and accuracy in reporting of any investigation is always pursued by our team of dedicated surveyors.
